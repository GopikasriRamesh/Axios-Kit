Alerts

Alerts provide contextual feedback to users about important actions or information. They come in four variants to cover common use cases.

<div class="alert alert--success">
  <p class="alert__text"><strong>Success!</strong> Your profile has been updated.</p>
</div>
<div class="alert alert--error">
  <p class="alert__text"><strong>Error!</strong> Please check the fields in red.</p>
</div>
<div class="alert alert--warning">
  <p class="alert__text"><strong>Warning!</strong> Your session will expire soon.</p>
</div>
<div class="alert alert--info">
  <p class="alert__text"><strong>Heads up!</strong> A new update is available.</p>
</div>
