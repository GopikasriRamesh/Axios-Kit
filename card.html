Card

Cards are used to group related information into a flexible, self-contained unit. They are a fundamental building block of modern interfaces.

<div class="card">
  <img class="card__image" src="..." alt="...">
  <div class="card__body">
    <h4 class="card__title">Card Title</h4>
    <p class="card__text">Some descriptive text.</p>
  </div>
</div>
<div class="card">
  <!-- ... card__image and card__body ... -->
  <div class="card__actions">
    <button class="btn btn--primary">Read More</button>
    <button class="btn">Share</button>
  </div>
</div>
